ICT Core Framework — All Pillars
🏗️
Market Structure
PILLAR 1 ▼
Market structure samajhna sabse pehla step hai. Bina structure ke koi entry nahi.
BULLISH STRUCTURE Higher High (HH) + Higher Low (HL) → Price ooncha ja raha hai, buy bias BEARISH STRUCTURE Lower Low (LL) + Lower High (LH) → Price neeche ja raha hai, sell bias BOS — Break of Structure Trend continue hoga signal Previous high/low toota = BOS CHOCH — Change of Character Trend reverse hone ki warning ⚠️ Opposite structure break = CHOCH

🎯 Rule: Hamesha HTF structure ke direction mein trade lo. Daily bullish hai toh 4H pe bhi buy setups dhundo.

💧
Liquidity Concepts
PILLAR 2 ▼
Yeh ICT ka heart hai. Banks retail traders ke stop losses use karte hain apne orders fill karne ke liye.
BSL — Buy Side Liquidity Equal highs ke upar = Short sellers ke SL Banks price upar le jaate hain → grab karte hain Fir sell karte hain wahan se 📉 SSL — Sell Side Liquidity Equal lows ke neeche = Long buyers ke SL Banks price neeche le jaate hain → grab karte hain Fir buy karte hain wahan se 📈 INDUCEMENT Fake move to attract retail traders Price quickly reverses after grab = Classic bank manipulation

🎯 Jab price equal highs/lows ke paas aaye toh alert ho jao. Liquidity grab ke baad reversal trade lo — yeh sabse powerful setup hai.

📐
PD Arrays — Premium & Discount
PILLAR 3 ▼
Price range ke andar positioning samajhna. Hamesha discount mein buy karo, premium mein sell karo.
EQUILIBRIUM (EQ) = 50% level Range = Swing High se Swing Low tak 50% ke upar = PREMIUM → Sell zone 50% ke neeche = DISCOUNT → Buy zone BULLISH TRADE Market bullish + Price in Discount = High probability BUY ✅ BEARISH TRADE Market bearish + Price in Premium = High probability SELL ✅ Use Fibonacci 0 to 1 0.5 = EQ, above = premium, below = discount

🎯 Kabhi bhi premium mein buy aur discount mein sell mat karo. Retail traders yahi mistake karte hain.

🧱
Order Blocks (OB)
PILLAR 4 ▼
Order blocks woh zones hain jahan banks ne apne orders place kiye the. Price wapas aata hai unhe fill karne.
BULLISH ORDER BLOCK Last bearish candle before a big bullish move = Bank buying was here Price wapas aye = BUY opportunity BEARISH ORDER BLOCK Last bullish candle before a big bearish move = Bank selling was here Price wapas aye = SELL opportunity OB STRENGTH Fresh OB > Used OB OB + FVG overlap = Very strong OB + S/R = Confluence = High probability MITIGATION Jab price OB pe touch kare = Mitigated After mitigation, OB weaker ho jaata hai

🎯 OB + Discount zone + Liquidity grab = ICT ki best entry. Yeh teen cheezein ek jagah milein toh sniper shot ready hai.

🌊
Order Flow
PILLAR 5 ▼
Candle by candle price ka behavior padhna. Kaise move kar raha hai — strong ya weak?
BULLISH ORDER FLOW ✅ Strong bullish candles (big body) ✅ Chhote pullbacks ✅ Pullbacks OB/FVG pe respected ✅ Lows protected (no SSL grab) BEARISH ORDER FLOW ✅ Strong bearish candles ✅ Chhote bounces ✅ Bounces OB/FVG pe rejected ✅ Highs protected (no BSL grab) ORDER FLOW SHIFT Strong bearish → Strong bullish engulfing = Early reversal signal ⚠️ Wait for structure confirmation
🕳️
Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
PILLAR 6 ▼
Price jahan se bahut fast move hua wahan gap hoti hai. Price wapas aata hai us gap ko fill karne.
FVG STRUCTURE (3 candles) Candle 1 → Candle 2 (big move) → Candle 3 BULLISH FVG Gap between: Candle 1 HIGH & Candle 3 LOW Price pullback into this zone = BUY ✅ BEARISH FVG Gap between: Candle 1 LOW & Candle 3 HIGH Price bounce into this zone = SELL ✅ IFVG — Inversion FVG Price fully fills FVG then reverses Used zone becomes opposite signal FVG + OB overlap = Strongest version

🎯 Fresh FVG strongest hoti hai. Pehli baar touch pe entry lo. Ek baar fill ho gayi toh uss zone ko delete karo chart se.

⚠️ The 4 Enemies of a Trader
😰
FOMO — Fear of Missing Out
▼

Price already move kar chuka hai, tujhe lag raha hai miss ho jaayega. Tum bina setup ke late entry lo. Yahi account killer hai.

💊 Fix: "Ek trade miss hua toh koi baat nahi. Market kal bhi khulega. Mera setup aayega."

😡
Revenge Trading
▼

Loss aaya, gussa aaya, bada trade liya wapas nikalne ke liye. Result? Aur bada loss.

💊 Fix: 2 consecutive losses = 30 min break mandatory. 3 losses = day band. No exceptions.

🤑
Greed — TP Hit ke Baad Bhi Hold
▼

TP1 hit hua, thoda aur chahiye. TP2 bhi, ab TP3 chahiye. Fir reversal — sab wapas chala gaya.

💊 Fix: Plan pehle banao. TP levels pehle set karo. TP hit hone pe partial close is non-negotiable.

🙏
Hope — SL Remove Karna
▼

"Wapas aayega" — yeh ek sentence ne traders ko barbad kiya hai. SL remove karna = account blow.

💊 Fix: SL = insurance policy. Kabhi remove mat karo. Loss ek chhota kharcha hai, SL nahi lagana ek bada disaster hai.

News Trading Guide — BTC & Gold
📅 High Impact Events

Gold pe sabse bada impact. Inflation data. High CPI = Gold up / Dollar confusion. Low CPI = Gold down.
Gold 🔥🔥🔥BTC 🔥🔥
FOMC — Fed Rate Decision
Federal Reserve interest rate decision. Rate up = Dollar up = Gold down. Rate down = Gold up. Market-moving event.
Gold 🔥🔥🔥BTC 🔥🔥🔥
NFP — Non-Farm Payroll
US jobs data. Strong jobs = Dollar strong = Gold weak. Weak jobs = Dollar weak = Gold strong. Every first Friday of month.
Gold 🔥🔥🔥Forex 🔥🔥🔥
FOMC Minutes
Fed meeting ke notes. Hawkish language = Dollar up = Gold down. Dovish = Gold up.
Gold 🔥🔥
BTC ETF News / Regulatory
ETF approval/rejection, country bans/approvals, whale movements. BTC-specific major catalyst.
BTC 🔥🔥🔥
🎯 News Trading Protocol
BEFORE NEWS (30 min) ❌ No new trades ❌ Existing trades mein SL tight karo ya close ✅ News event note karo ✅ Expected direction samjho DURING NEWS (0–5 min) ❌ Bilkul mat trade karo ❌ Spreads bahut wide ho jaate hain ❌ Slippage risk bahut high ✅ Sirf dekho, samjho AFTER NEWS (5–15 min) ✅ Volatility settle hone do ✅ Initial spike often reverses ✅ Real direction 10-15 min baad clear ✅ Fir ICT setup dhundo news direction mein PRO TIP "Buy the rumor, sell the news" Expected news already priced in hoti hai Surprise news = biggest moves
🔗 DXY — Gold Relationship
DXY vs GOLD (Almost Always Inverse) DXY ⬆️ Up → Gold ⬇️ Down DXY ⬇️ Down → Gold ⬆️ Up Risk Sentiment Risk-ON → BTC up, Gold sideways Risk-OFF → Gold up, BTC down Crisis → Both drop, then Gold recovers PRACTICAL RULE Daily chart pe DXY dekho DXY bearish week = Gold buy setups dhundo DXY bullish week = Gold sell setups dhundo
